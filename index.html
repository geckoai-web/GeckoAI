Workflow

A product workflow designed for real factory and QA environments.

Step 1

Import Images and Datasets

Supports plain image folders, YOLO datasets, golden packages, and camera-captured sources.

Step 2

Label and Define Standards

Create classes, refine OBB annotations, and export golden packages to establish a repeatable inspection baseline.

Step 3

Train and Deploy Models

Automatically aligns with YOLO or YOLO-OBB based on dataset format while keeping CPU and GPU flexibility.

Step 4

Inspect, Compare, and Report

Run golden comparison, OCR traceability, and dashboard reporting to make review and follow-up much clearer.

Use Cases

The same platform can be adapted to very different industrial inspection tasks.

Coffee Bag Packaging Inspection

Verify packaging completeness, label placement, seal areas, and valve presence against expected standards.

  • Golden comparison for layout consistency
  • Pass-fail reasons flow directly into reports

Coffee Bean Grading

Count broken beans, black beans, insect damage, and foreign materials to build structured quality datasets.

  • Model detection with class-level statistics
  • Useful for QA and procurement review

PCBA Defect Inspection

Combine background cutting, piece inspection, golden comparison, and OCR to localize missing or shifted components.

  • Supports board-level and piece-level analysis
  • Writes ID and sub-ID into traceability reports
